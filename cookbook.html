Rootin Tootin Chili Cheese Fries

  • Time: 45min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Potatos 4 lbs
Salt 1 tbsp
Black Pepper 1 tbsp
Oil 1 tbsp
Chili with beans 15 oz
Crushed Red Pepper 1/2 tbsp
Shredded Cheese 8 oz

Step 1: Potato Prep

  1. Scrub potatos under cold water until clean of dirt.
  2. Longways, cut the sides off the potatos to make a rectangle with round ends
  3. Cut potatos in to fry sizes of your preference and place in cold water
  4. After all the potatos have been cut in and placed in the cold water, stir fries in water 3 or 4 time and pour out starch soaked water and replace with clean cold water and let soak for 10 mins and preheat oven to 450 degrees
  5. Drain water and pat potatos dry with paper towels
  6. Return to bowl, add the tbsp of Salt, Black Pepper, and oil, then mix by hand until all fries are well covered(longer the better)
  7. Spray baking sheet with favorite cooking spray and spread fries on sheet and cook for 35-40 mins

Step 2: Chili Prep

  1. About 10 mins before fries are done baking, place the 15 oz of chili w/beans in to a pot and heat up on low temp
  2. Stir in the 1/2 tbsp of Crushed Red Pepper
  3. Keep on low heat, stir occasionally until fries are done

Step 3: It all comes together

  1. Turn oven off and remove fries
  2. spread chili w/beans over the fries
  3. Sprinkle shredded on top of it all and then place everything in oven to let the cheese melt
  4. When cheese is melted(about 5 mins) remove from oven and enjoy
